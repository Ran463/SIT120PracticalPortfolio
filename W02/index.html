Fun Facts about Pandas

Pandas have an amazing capability to camouflage accordingly to their habitat. This is because their fur has two basic functions: camouflage and communication. The white fur on their bodies are to hide them in snowy conditions, whereas the black can assist them in keeping hidden in the shade. Additionally, their eyes contain a similar attribute to that of cats, where in their pupils are in the form of vertical slits. Moreover, did you know that one panda year is equivalent to three of our human years!
